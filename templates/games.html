GAMES

Below is a list of the most popular games in your area. Go out, play and have some fun!

Toros Center

Houston Toros is an outdoor turf futsal soccer field. Perfect for field rentals, party rentals, and the best Futsal leagues in town. Also be sure to check out our famous $5 Open play on Fridays from 8-10pm!

Memorial Park

Memorial Park, a municipal park in Houston, Texas, is one of the largest urban parks in the United States.

Burroughs Park

Burroughs Park, provides children and adults of all skill levels the chance to play competitive and fun soccer.

Main Street indoor soccer

A Downtown Houston indoor soccer facility offering soccer leagues and training for adults, children, as well as corporate.

Eagle Creek Sports Complex

Come play on the state-of-the-art in-fill turf field at Houston's only indoor soccer arena inside the Loop. They offer all the amenities you expect from a first-class arena.

Lyndsay Lyons

Located in Atascocita, Texas 136-acre complex with lighted baseball, soccer, football & soccer fields, playground & picnic areas..

Houston Sports Park

Center of Excellence programs offer advanced training to elite male and female players aged 8-12 and provide high-level supplementary training and the first step into the Houston Dynamo Academy.

Bear Creek Park

A Greater Houston, non-profit, youth baseball and softball park dedicated to providing a fun, safe, exciting and nurturing place where children can develop skills.
