Welcome to Piqup!

Play Football in interesting places. Share experiences with your friends. Simplify the game.
  • Soccer

Features

Uniforms

No more 'Shirts'/'Skins'! Stay organized by finding out what colors to wear to any game in your city!

Rosters

Sort out rosters and pick teams before the game so you can start on-time!

Ball Up!

Tired of showing up to the game and no one has a ball? Stay organized through Piqup and message your friends to find out who is bringing it!

Twitter Integration

Tweet with frends about games in your area. Share results, photos, game times, and start #hashtags for your games!

Google Maps support

Find games in your area through Google's powerful Map API. With 'street view' you can get an idea of where the game is, and what the playing field or street looks like!

Rate Games

Let other users know about the conditions of the game that you played in.
